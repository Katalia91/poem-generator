AI Running Plan Generator

Welcome to the Running Plan Generator!

Just type in the distance you’re training for and how many times per week you can train (e.g. “10 km, 3x per week”), and I’ll create a personalized running plan tailored to your needs.

Happy running! 🏃‍♀️

This tool can generate up to 4 weeks of a training plan at a time.
i.e. "5km, 3x week", "marathon, 4x week"
